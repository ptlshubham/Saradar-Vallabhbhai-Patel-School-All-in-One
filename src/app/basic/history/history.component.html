About Us
Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel High School, Khetiwadi(HS)

We have created an atmosphere that encourages free thought among the teacher, the teacher's parents, the principal, the teacher's management, and the student's management. Our school is well equipped with up-to-date laboratories, the latest computer room, a well-stocked library, and sports instruments to facilitate the teaching and learning process. Our huge playground contributes to the physical development and recreation of our students. Our school is covered by green trees and clean play grounds.
